The Glass Orchard

A family returns to their childhood orchard to settle an inheritance — and the secrets buried beneath it.

Amazon Barnes & Noble Bookshop.org

When Eleanor Voss returns to the orchard her grandmother left behind, she expects paperwork and polite arguments with her brother over what to keep and what to sell. What she finds instead is a locked toolshed, a decades-old letter never sent, and a version of her family's history no one agreed to remember the same way. A slow-burn story about inheritance — the kind you can hold, and the kind you can't put down.

Salt & Static

When the signal towers along the coast start broadcasting voices that shouldn't exist, one engineer has to find out why.

Amazon Apple Books

The towers along the coast have always hummed with routine signal traffic — until they start broadcasting voices that match no known frequency, and no living person. Engineer Mara Doyle is sent to fix what looks like a malfunction, but the deeper she gets into the system, the less it looks like an accident, and the less certain she becomes that the voices are asking to be silenced.

About

Hi, I'm Parker Shelton.

I was born in Arkansas and grew up moving around a bit before landing in Charlotte, North Carolina, where I'm based today.

I've always been a creative person before I was ever a writer — drawing, making music, chasing whatever medium would hold still long enough to let something out. Writing found me the same way, in the free hours between everything else, until it became the thing I couldn't put down. I write across literary fiction, thriller and suspense, and sci-fi.

None of it means much without my two daughters. They're the reason behind everything I do, on the page and off it.

  • Based in — Charlotte, North Carolina
  • From — Arkansas
  • Genres — literary fiction, thriller & suspense, sci-fi
  • Also creates — visual art and music
