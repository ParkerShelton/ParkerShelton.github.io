Psychological Thriller

Lucid

A lifelong lucid dreamer discovers he isn't alone in his dreams — and the stranger invading them is real.

Coming soon

Reid has been a lucid dreamer his whole life — in sleep, he shapes entire worlds and walks through buildings before he ever designs them. It's the one place he has always felt completely safe, and completely in control. Then, one night, someone else is there: a terrified stranger who looks at him and asks, "I thought this was my dream. How are you here?" After that, Reid's dreams stop obeying him — until the day he sees that same stranger step out of a hospital in the waking world, alive and real. A psychological thriller about consciousness, control, and how far a person will go to escape the parts of themselves they can't face.

Young Adult Sci-Fi

The Hollow

In a city built so no one looks too closely, one watchful teenager finds an object that makes the whole world go quiet.

Coming soon

Seventeen-year-old Ash Callen has always noticed what his city is built to keep people from noticing — the filtered air, the manufactured happiness, the quiet machinery of a world optimized so no one looks too closely. Then an old man watches him from a bench and leaves behind a small, strange object covered in markings he can't read — and when Ash holds it, the noise of the city pulls back and the whole world goes quiet. Soon a substitute teacher who knows far too much tells him something that changes everything: he's been doing it his whole life, without knowing. Alongside his sharp-eyed best friend Rook, Ash is pulled into a hidden layer of his own world, racing to understand what the object is, what he is, and what the people watching him really want — before the gray, ordinary city he's spent his life seeing through decides what to do with someone who can finally see all of it. A young-adult sci-fi mystery about perception, control, and the cost of finally seeing clearly.

Children's Fantasy

The Pink Bear Protocols

Millie thinks she's just playing outside — but the woods behind her apartment have been waiting for her all along.

Coming soon

When Millie plays outside, she's just having fun. At least, that's what she tells her dad. But on the hill behind her apartment, something magical is waiting — a deer with impossible antlers, a wolf who forgot how to howl, a little purple dragon who needs a friend. With her trusty (and very opinionated) companion Pink Bear along for every adventure, Millie is about to discover that the woods have been waiting for her all along. A warm, funny chapter book about kindness, curiosity, and seeing the magic everyone else walks right past.

About

Hi, I'm Parker Shelton.

I was born in Arkansas and grew up moving around a bit before landing in Charlotte, North Carolina, where I'm based today.

I've always been a creative person before I was ever a writer — drawing, making music, chasing whatever medium would hold still long enough to let something out. Writing found me the same way, in the free hours between everything else, until it became the thing I couldn't put down. I write across literary fiction, thriller and suspense, and sci-fi.

None of it means much without my two daughters. They're the reason behind everything I do, on the page and off it.

  • From — Arkansas
  • Genres — literary fiction, thriller & suspense, sci-fi
  • Also creates — visual art and music
